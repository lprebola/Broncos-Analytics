Key Insights

Bo Nix Year 2 Leap

Nix improved from 3,775 passing yards in 2024 to 4,210 in 2025, showing clear sophomore growth and increased efficiency.

Courtland Sutton Consistency

Sutton posted back-to-back 1,000+ yard seasons, proving to be the most reliable weapon in the Broncos offense.

RJ Harvey Breakout

Rookie RJ Harvey totaled 1,021 yards from scrimmage and 12 TDs, immediately becoming a key part of the offense.

Troy Franklin Emergence

Franklin jumped from 263 receiving yards in 2024 to 709 in 2025, establishing himself as Nix's second option.

Balanced Passing Attack

5 receivers surpassed 400 receiving yards in 2025, showing the Broncos effectively spread the ball across the roster.

Evan Engram Addition

New addition Evan Engram provided 487 receiving yards at TE, a position that lacked production in 2024.

SQL Queries Used

-- Chart 1: Receiving yards 2024 vs 2025 (top 6 WR/TE by 2025 yards)
SELECT s25.player_display_name, s25.position,
  s24.receiving_yards AS yards_2024,
  s25.receiving_yards AS yards_2025,
  (s25.receiving_yards - s24.receiving_yards) AS yards_change
FROM broncos_stats s25
JOIN broncos_stats_2024 s24 ON s25.player_id = s24.player_id
WHERE s25.position IN ('WR','TE')
ORDER BY s25.receiving_yards DESC
FETCH FIRST 6 ROWS ONLY;
-- Chart 2: Total touchdowns by player 2025 (top 7 skill players)
SELECT player_display_name, position,
  receiving_tds, rushing_tds, passing_tds,
  (receiving_tds + rushing_tds + passing_tds) AS total_tds
FROM broncos_stats
WHERE position IN ('WR','RB','TE','QB')
ORDER BY total_tds DESC
FETCH FIRST 7 ROWS ONLY;
-- Chart 3: RB yards from scrimmage 2025 (top 5 RBs)
SELECT player_display_name,
  rushing_yards,
  receiving_yards,
  (rushing_yards + receiving_yards) AS total_yards_from_scrimmage
FROM broncos_stats
WHERE position = 'RB'
ORDER BY total_yards_from_scrimmage DESC
FETCH FIRST 5 ROWS ONLY;
-- Chart 4: Bo Nix year over year comparison
SELECT s25.player_display_name,
  s24.passing_yards AS passing_yards_2024,
  s25.passing_yards AS passing_yards_2025,
  s24.passing_tds AS passing_tds_2024,
  s25.passing_tds AS passing_tds_2025,
  s24.rushing_yards AS rushing_yards_2024,
  s25.rushing_yards AS rushing_yards_2025
FROM broncos_stats s25
JOIN broncos_stats_2024 s24 ON s25.player_id = s24.player_id
WHERE s25.player_display_name = 'Bo Nix';
-- Chart 5: Position group touchdown breakdown 2025
SELECT position,
  SUM(receiving_tds) AS receiving_tds,
  SUM(rushing_tds) AS rushing_tds,
  SUM(passing_tds) AS passing_tds,
  SUM(receiving_tds + rushing_tds + passing_tds) AS total_tds
FROM broncos_stats
WHERE position IN ('QB','WR','RB','TE')
GROUP BY position
ORDER BY total_tds DESC;
